RESEARCH

My research interests can be broadly categorized into three topics.

Quantum Entanglement

I study quantum entanglement in quantum many-body systems and quantum field theory, which extends the framework of quantum mechanics.

Non-equilibrium Quantum Dynamics

Non-equilibrium and open quantum systems often exhibit novel and counterintuitive phenomena. I investigate quantum dynamics using theoretical physics and quantum information theory.

Applications of Quantum Computing

I explore applications of quantum computing to quantum many-body systems and quantum field theory, aiming to solve problems that are difficult for classical computation.
